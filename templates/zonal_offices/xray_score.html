Scoring Legend:

i. Pass Mark is 50
ii. Score grade = (50 – 59 marks is for Provisional Registration, while above 60marks is for Full Registration where one modality is considered); For a facility with more than one modality, failure in any of the modalities or score of 50 – 59marks in all the modalities shall attract issuing of Provisional Registration of Practice Certificate pending the remedy of the defects.
iii.Each modality should have at least 2 Radiographers (50 -59marks for less than two per modality)

1. Shielding of the Equipment room, door, window, and console area with lead, barium plaster or concrete of adequate thickness is considered as (wall = 6marks, door/door & window = 5marks, and console area = 4 marks).
2. Room design and layout for all radiation emitting equipment should be in strict compliance to high radiation safety standards
3. Each Radiographer attracts 2.5 marks
4. Each Radiologist is 1.5 marks
5. Each current licence is 2.5 marks
6. PRMD (TLDs & Instadose) = 5marks; PRPE (Lead apron, Lead Shield, Console Lead glass of adequate Lead equivalent, Lead glasses, Lead gloves etc) = 5 marks
7. Non Radiographer RSO, and Non Radiographer RSA = 1mark, Radiographer RSO and Non Radiographer RSA = 1.5marks, while Radiographer RSO and Radiographer RSA = 2 marks
8. Running water (5 marks) is preferable source of water supply than stagnant water – water in reservoir or container (2 marks)
9. Equipment certification must be by authority recognizable in Nigeria
10. Each conventional X-Ray accessory attracts 1 mark
11. Warning light must be mounted on entrance to radiation equipment room.
12. Warning signs must be placed on entrance to radiation equipment room. It must be in lingua franca and local dialect
13. The completed form CO7 should contain names of the directors and their contacts
14. For Adequacy of Equipment, Installation & Location: Conventional X-Ray with output below 100kV = 1mark, while >100kV = 2marks; Proper Installation (Where X-Ray tube is not directed to public/residential zone or such a way that radiation may seriously hit the console area) = 1mark, & Proper Location (I.e. ground floor) = 1 mark. Only Functional Equipment is to be considered.
15. Processing unit includes (a) manual = 3marks to be distributed as follows (each tank = 0.3 mark, Standard Dryer = 0.3 mark, wet bench = 0.3 mark., Dry bench = 0.3 mark, actinic marker 0.3 mark, safelight = 0.3 mark, hatch = 0.3 mark, film carrier = 0.2 mark, film storage system = 0.3mark, hangers & cassettes = 0.4);
(b) Automatic Processing = 3.5marks to be distributed as follows (automatic processing machine = 1.5marks, Dry bench = 0.3 mark, actinic marker 0.3mark, safelight = 0.3mark, hatch = 0.3mark, film carrier = 0.3mark, film storage system = 0.2mark, hangers & cassettes = 0.3);
(c) Digitized processing = 4 marks
16. Toilets include General Toilet (2.5mark), & Staff Toilet (2.5marks)
17. Adequacy of waiting room should comprise of its cleanliness (1.5 marks), ventilation/air conditioning (1.5 marks) and furnishing (2 marks)
18. Offices include Office of Radiographer-in-Charge (2 marks), & Radiographers’ Common Room (2 marks)
