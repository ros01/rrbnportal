Scoring Legend:


i. Pass Mark is 50
ii. Score grade = (50 – 59marks is for Provisional Registration, while above 60marks is for Full Registration where one modality is considered); For a facility with more than one modality, failure in any of the modalities or score of 50 – 59marks in all the modalities shall attract issuing of Provisional Registration of Practice Certificate pending the remedy of the defects.
iii. Each modality should have at least 2 Radiographers (50 -59marks for less than two per modality)
1. Shielding of the Equipment room, door, window, and console area with lead, barium plaster or concrete of adequate thickness is considered as (wall = 4marks, door/door & window = 3marks, and console area = 3marks). Shield for radiation source must be of multi-leaf collimators.
2. Arrangement of the reception to the treatment room must meet up to the recommended lead-line/concrete thickness
3. The number of Radiographers with respect to a radiotherapy facility in ideal situation should be 2 in the Mould Room, 2 in CT Room and 4 in the Radiotherapy Machine Room. Minimum of 4 must be available. Each Radiographer attracts 2.5marks
4. Each Radiologist is 2mark
5. Other staff include Medical Physicist (0.5mark), Oncology nurse (0.5mark), Oncology Pharmacist (0.5mark), Medical Record Staff/Departmental Secretary (0.3mark), Biomedical Engineer (0.2mark)
6. Post graduate certification in radiotherapy must be that recognized and registrable by RRBN
7. Each current licence is 2.5marks
8. PRMD (TLDs & Instadose) & PRMD reading frequency & return = 5 marks; PRPE (Lead apron, Lead Shield, Console Lead glass of adequate Lead equivalent, Lead glasses, Lead gloves = 5 marks.
9. Non Radiographer RSO and Non Radiographer RSA = 1mark, Radiographer RSO and Non Radiographer RSA = 1.5marks, while Radiographer RSO and Radiographer RSA = 2marks
10. Running water (5marks) is preferable source of water supply than stagnant water – water in reservoir or container (2marks)
11. Equipment certification must be by authority recognizable in Nigeria
12. Warning light must be mounted on entrance to radiation equipment room. Warning lights = 2marks, warning lights and lighting synergies = 4marks
13. Warning signs must be placed on entrance to radiation equipment room. It must be in lingua franca and local dialect
14. The completed form CO7 should contain names of the directors and their contacts
15. The installation and location of all radiation equipment should be made in strict compliance to high radiation safety standards
16. Each radiotherapy accessory attracts 1mark
17. The Mould room must have a stand for casting, mask should be labelled with expiry date, mould tools and shelves for storage should be adequate and secured
18. Toilets include toilet for patient (2.5marks) and toilet for staff (2.5marks)
19. Adequacy of waiting room should comprise of its cleanliness (1.5marks), ventilation/air conditioning (1.5marks) and furnishing (2marks)
20. Offices include Office of Radiographer-in-Charge (1mark), & Radiographers’ Common Room (1mark), Clinical consulting areas (1mark), medical Physicist office (0.5mark), Radiotherapy Nurse office (0.5mark), Oncology Pharmacist Office (0.5mark), Medical Record Office/Secretary Office (0.5mark)
