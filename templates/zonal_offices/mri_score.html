Scoring Legend:

i. Pass Mark is 50
ii. Score grade = (50 – 59 marks is for Provisional Registration, while above 60marks is for Full Registration where one modality is considered); For a facility with more than one modality, failure in any of the modalities or score of 50 – 59 marks in all the modalities shall attract issuing of Provisional Registration of Practice Certificate pending the remedy of the defects.
iii.Each modality should have at least 2 Radiographers (50 -59 marks for less than two per modality)

1. Shielding of the relevant parts of the equipment room with Faraday Cage must be properly done
2. Design and layout of the treatment room must be in such a way to guarantee safety of patients & staff
3. Each Radiographer attracts 2.5 marks
4. Each Radiologist attracts 1.5 marks
5. Each current licence of the Radiographer attracts 2.5marks
6. Post graduate certification in MRI must be that recognized and registrable by RRBN
7. Availability Metal Screening Device = 2 marks; Availability of Metal Screening Device and evidence of proper use = 4 marks
8. Availability Screening Questionnaire = 2 marks; Availability of Metal Screening Device and evidence of proper use = 4 marks
9. Running water (2marks) is preferable source of water supply than stagnant water – water in reservoir or container (1 mark)
10. Each MRI accessory attracts 1mark
11. Warning signs must be placed on entrance to MRI equipment room. It must be in lingua franca and local dialect
12. The completed form CO7 should contain names of the directors and their contacts
13. For Adequacy of Equipment, Installation & Location: MRI with strength below 1.5T = 3 marks, while >1.5T = 6marks; Proper Installation = 2marks & Proper Location = 2marks. Only Functional Equipment is to be considered.
14. Good processing unit = 1mark; Good processing unit linked with teleradiology, PACS or the like = 2 marks
15. Toilets include General Toilet (1mark), & Staff Toilet (1 mark)
16. Adequacy of waiting room should comprise of its cleanliness (1.5marks), ventilation/air conditioning (1.5marks) and furnishing (2 marks)
17. Offices include Office of Radiographer-in-Charge (2marks), & Radiographers’ Common Room (2 marks)
18. Technical Room include rooms for backup power (eg batteries) = 2 marks, and room for maintenance personnel = 1 mark
