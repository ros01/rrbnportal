Scoring Legend:

i. Pass Mark is 50
ii. Score grade = (50 – 59 marks is for Provisional Registration, while above 60marks is for Full Registration where one modality is considered); For a facility with more than one modality, failure in any of the modalities or score of 50 – 59 marks in all the modalities shall attract issuing of Provisional Registration of Practice Certificate pending the remedy of the defects.
iii. Each modality should have at least 2 Radiographers (50 -59marks for less than two per modality)

1. Shielding of the Equipment room, door, window, and console area with lead, barium plaster or concrete of adequate thickness is considered as (wall = 6 marks, door/door & window = 5 marks, and console area = 4 marks). Shield for radiation source must be of multi-leaf collimators.
2. Room design and layout for all radiation emitting equipment should be in strict compliance to high radiation safety standards
3. Each Radiographer attracts 2.5 marks
4. Each Radiologist is 1.5 marks
5. Each current licence is 2.5 marks
6. Post graduate certification in Angiography/Cath Lab must be that recognized and registrable by RRBN
7. PRMD (TLDs & Instadose) = 5 marks; PRPE (Lead apron, Lead Shield, Console Lead glass of adequate Lead equivalent, Lead glasses, Lead gloves etc) = 5 marks
8. Non Radiographer RSO, and Non Radiographer RSA = 1 mark, Radiographer RSO and Non Radiographer RSA = 1.5 marks, while Radiographer RSO and Radiographer RSA = 2 marks
9. Running water (2 marks) is preferable source of water supply than stagnant water – water in reservoir or container 1 marks
10. Equipment certification must be by authority recognizable in Nigeria
11. For Angiography Machine/Cath Lab each viewing monitor attracts 1 mark (1 for user & 1 for patient). Other accessories attract 0.5 mark each
12. Warning light must be mounted on entrance to radiation equipment room.
13. Warning signs must be placed on entrance to radiation equipment room. It must be in lingua franca and local dialect
14. The completed form CO7 should contain names of the directors and their contacts
15. For Adequacy of Equipment, Installation & Location: Good angiography/Cath Lab equipment = 4 marks. Proper installation = 1 mark, & Proper Location (i.e. ground floor) = 1 marks. Only Functional Equipment is to be considered.
16. Good processing unit = 2 marks; Good processing unit linked with teleradiology, PACS or the like = 4marks 17. Toilets include General Toilet (2 marks), & Staff Toilet (2 marks)
18. Adequacy of waiting room should comprise of its cleanliness (1.5 marks), ventilation/air conditioning (1.5 marks) and furnishing (2 marks)
19. Offices include Office of Radiographer-in-Charge (2 marks), & Radiographers’ Common Room (2 marks)
