About RRBN

The Radiographers Registration Boadr of Nigeria (RRBN) is a parastatal under the ministry of health.RRBN was established by Decree N0. 42 of 1987, now Cap R1 Laws of the Fedration of Nigeria 2004.RRBN key mandate is to regulate the price of Radiography in Nigeria.
