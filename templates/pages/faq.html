Frequently Asked Questions

How can I change my password after successful login?
  1. Login into the Portal
  2. Click on the drop-down arrow beside your name at the top right of your screen.
  3. Select Account Setting
  4. On the Account screen, click on the Change Password button.
  5. In the Change Password dialog box which appears, type your new password in the New Password box, and again in the Confirm Password box.
  6. Click the Change button.
What is the RRBN Monitoring Portal?

The RRBN Monitoring and Enforcement Portal is an Electronic Platform for Data Consolidation of all major activities carried out by the Monitoring and Enforcement Department of the RRBN in its bid to uphold the mandate of the Board to regulate the Practice of Radiography in Nigeria.

RRBN Monitoring and Enforcement Portal provides a repository of easily retrievable data of all hospitals dispensing ionizing radation, their addresses, list of equipment, radiographers covering the hospital,practice registration/compliance status, registration and renewal module for ease of registrations/renewals as well as an inspections module for inspections scheduling and real time upload of inspection observations/reports.

Who Can Register on the portal?

Categories of those who can Register on the RRBN Monitoring Portalinclude:

  1. Diagnostic Centre Administrators
  2. Hospital Administrators
  3. Radiography Training Institutions
  4. Research Institutions
I forgot my password?
  1. Go to the Login Page, click on the link 'forgot password'.
  2. Type the email address which you used to create the account.
  3. Click 'Send New Password'
  4. A New Password will be sent to your email address.
Is there any financial involvement in registering one's practice on the RRBN Monitoring and Enforcement portal?

Yes. A mandatory registration/renewal fee of N50,000 is required. Renewal is on an annual basis.

What are the necessary Requirements for Registration of Radiography Practice with RRBN.
  1. Application letter register your practice addressed to the Registrar of RRBN.
  2. Certificate of incorporation of the company (evidence of registration of business name is also acceptable).
  3. Payment of prescribed fee of N50,000.
  4. Form C07 showing names and particulars of director(s) and radiographer(s).
  5. Letter of undertaking by the managing director of the company to the effect that all matters pertaining to Radiography and Radiography business shall be under the direct management of the Radiographer in-charge.
  6. Staff Requirements: a. Current Radiography practisinglicence/evidence of payment from Radiographer in-charge (and other radiographers if applicable) b. Letter of resignation from previous employment (from the Radiographer in-charge, if applicable) c. NYSC discharge or exemption certificate (from Radiographer in-charge).
When would one expect email from RRBN scheduling them for inspection after completion of basic registration processes?

RRBN undertakes to process all registration processes as soon as possible, typically within 2 weeks of submission of request for registration/renewal of practice.
