Benefits of the RRBN M&E Portal

The RRBN Monitoring and Enforcement Portal completely automates all core processes involved in the application and renewal of Radiography Practice in Nigeria within the Radiographers Registration Board of Nigeria. With the Portal in place, Hospitals, Institutions and Diagnostic centre Administrators can register or renew the practices from the comfort of the hospitals or centres. Listed below is a summary of benefits of the portal:

  •  Convenient and Easy to Use
  •   High System Security
  •   e-Payment
  •   Electronic Data Storage and Processing
  •   Paperless, Flexible and Robust
  •  Real time Notifications and Alerts
  •  Numerous Reports for Administrative Purposes
  •  A Digital Experience Platform
