Benefits of the RRBN Inspection and Monitoring Portal

The RRBN Inspection and Monitoring Portal completely automates all core processes involved in the application and renewal of Radiography Practice License/Accreditation for Internship in Nigeria with the Radiographers Registration Board of Nigeria. With the Portal in place, Hospitals, Institutions and Diagnostic Center Administrators can register or renew their Radiography practices from the comfort of their hospitals or centers. Listed below is a summary of benefits of the portal:

  •  Convenient and Easy to Use
  •   High System Security
  •   Electronic Data Storage and Processing
  •   Paperless, Flexible and Robust
  •  Real time Notifications and Alerts
  •  Numerous Reports for Administrative Purposes
  •  A Digital Experience Platform
