Notice: This is the first step for New Hospitals/Centres Registration with the Radiographers Registration Board of Nigeria. Kindly go through the guildlines and conditions. Please kindly have ready, along with other specified documents, evidence of payment of the registration fee of N50,000 as you will need to upload them for verification before your registration can be confirmed.

Requirements for Hospitals Registration

  1. Valid Certificate of Incorporation.

  2. Form C07 showing names and particulars of director(s) and radiographer(s).

  3. Current Radiographer Practicing Licence.

  4. Evidence of Payment of fifty thousand naira (N50,000.00) Registration fee through remita

    5. Account Details: 1000032307

Note: Provisional Registration is valid for 2 years.

Registration Process Flow:

  • STEP 1: Hospital Practice Manager Profile Creation

  • STEP 2: Submission of basic hospital/centre details, certificate of incorporation, form C07, evidence of payment of N50,000 registration fee and radiographer practicing Licence.

  • STEP 3: Verification of submitted documents and registration fee details.

  • STEP 4: Upon succesful verification of submitted documents and payment details, hospital/centre is scheduled for inspection.

  • STEP 5: Facility inspection is carried out by RRBN Inspection and monitoring team from the Head Quarters or Zonal offices.

  • STEP 6: Report of facility inspection communicated to hospital with summary of areas of improvement.

  • STEP 7: Hospital issued Provisional Practice permit valid for 2 years.

Note: Upon issue of provisional permit, Hospital/centre is informed of next date of inspection, monioring. This is required of issue of full registration certificate/accreditation for internship.
