Radiography Practice Permit Renewal - Guidelines Page

Kindly go through the guidelines and conditions. Annual Radiography Practice Permit Renewal Fee is N50,000.00

Requirements for Radiography Practice Permit Renewal [Download]

  1. Valid Certificate of Incorporation
  2. Form C07 showing names and particulars of director(s) and radiographer(s)
  3. Radiographer In Charge Passport Photograph
  4. Radiographer In Charge Current Practice Licence
  5. Payment of Radiography Practice Permit renewal fee of (N50,000.00) to be paid to RRBN TSA Account:
    Name of MDA: Radiographers Registration Board - 052100600100
    Name of Service/Purpose: Renewal of Practice Permit Fee
    Bank: All Banks


NOTE: Radiography Practice Permit is valid for One (1) Year

Application Process Flow

  1. STAGE 1: Hospital Profile Creation (for first time users of the platform). Already registered users should login to their dashboard to start practice permit renewal
  2. STAGE 2: Submission of basic hospital/centre details, certificate of incorporation, form C07, Radiographer In Charge passport and Practice Licence and evidence of payment of cost of available modalities.
  3. STAGE 3: Verification of submitted documents and modalities fees payment.
  4. STAGE 4: Final Approval by RRBN Registrar/CEO.
  5. STAGE 5: Radiography Practice Permit Issuance
