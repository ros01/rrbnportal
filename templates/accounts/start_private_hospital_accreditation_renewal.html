Internship Accreditation Renewal Guidelines Page

Kindly go through the guildlines and conditions. You will need to upload your evidence of payment of the accreditation fee for verification before your hospital can be scheduled for accreditation inspection. Internship accreditations for Private Hospitals attract a Accreditation Fee of N100,000 plus N50,000 each for a minimum of two modalities. Additional modalities attract further N50,000 per listed modality.

Requirements for New Private Hospital Accreditation [Download]

  1. Current Radiographer Practicing Licence
  2. Prescribed fee of (N100,000.00 plus N50,000 per listed modality (minimum of two)) to be paid to RRBN TSA Account:
    Name of MDA: Radiographers Registration Board - 052100600100
    Name of Service/Purpose: Accreditation Fee
    Bank: All Banks


NOTE: Provisional Accreditation is valid for Two (2) Years while Full Accrdeditation is valid for Four (4) Years

Application Process Flow

  1. STAGE 1: Hospital Profile Creation
  2. STAGE 2: Submission of basic hospital/ centre details, evidence of payment of accreditation fee and radiographer practicing Licence.
  3. STAGE 3: Verification of submitted document(s) and accreditation fee details.
  4. STAGE 4: Facility Inspection.
  5. STAGE 5: Full or Provisional Accreditation
